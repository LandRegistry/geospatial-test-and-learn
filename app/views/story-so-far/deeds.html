What is the problem?

Test and Learn are looking to solve the “Copy Filed issue” by extracting data from deeds for entry directly onto the Land Register.

“Copy Filed” is where a deed is lodged for registration and contains information (easements, covenants, restrictions) that requires entry on the register and only a short form entry is made on the register itself stating that a copy of the deed has been filed, whilst the deed itself has been retained by HMLR as a complete document. This means the register does not contain full details of interests affecting property.

This practice causes issues for customers (who are required to purchase copies of deeds to understand the full details of the register) and presents a challenge to the development of a Geospatial Register (where, potentially, all information is presented together on a map).

Test and Learn’s Data Science team will investigate suitable methodologies to extract the data from retained deeds - for inclusion on a potential Geospatial Register.
