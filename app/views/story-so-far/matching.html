Matching textual and spatial data

DRAFT CONTENT

HMLR's current data is held separately as textual and spatial data. One of the features in Test and Learn is investigating methods to match extual and spatial register entries and create a link between the two.

GTL’s Data Science team are developing an automated method to create digital links between textual register entries references on the Casework Mapping System (CMS)

In addition, an Annotation Tool is being developed to enable the provision of a “gold standard dataset” that has been matched manually - to quality assure the digital method against.

What did we do?

  • Build a new environment in Amazon sage maker
  • Import spatial and textual data for 250,00 titles
  • Publish a data model on OPD
  • Imported Spatial data from DB2 and transformed from TMF
  • Selected 3 LA Areas based on their make-up – Manchester (urban), S Gloucs (Rural) Bournemouth, Christchurch and Poole (mixed and coastal)
  • Obtained textual data from IAP and copied to our SW storage to enable full text view.
